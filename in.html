TechFusion 2025

TechFusion 2025 is a dynamic two-day extravaganza dedicated to fueling the innovative spirit of B.Tech Computer Science students and technology enthusiasts. Set to become the ultimate hub for creativity and collaboration, this event brings together coding aficionados, AI/ML pioneers, cybersecurity wizards, blockchain enthusiasts, and budding tech entrepreneurs under one roof. Designed as a platform to explore cutting-edge technologies, exchange groundbreaking ideas, and showcase talent, TechFusion 2025 offers a plethora of opportunities to learn, network, and grow. Whether you are passionate about crafting mobile applications, developing immersive games, delving into blockchain technology, mastering the art of data science, or strengthening your expertise in cybersecurity, this event has something tailored just for you.

Hackathon Rounds

  • Code Blitz – Speed coding challenge with time constraints
  • Bugged Out – Debugging and optimizing faulty code
  • Build & Conquer – Develop a working prototype based on a given problem statement

Quiz Rounds

  • Tech Tussle – Rapid-fire tech trivia
  • Code Chronicles – Solve code snippets and algorithms
  • Logic Lords – Puzzle-solving and logical reasoning challenges
  • The Final Firewall – Cybersecurity and network-related quiz

Workshops & Interactive Sessions

  • Arena – Hands-on with machine learning models
  • CloudScape – Workshop on cloud computing and deployment
  • Cyber Quest – Ethical hacking and penetration testing basics
  • Web Wizardry – Full-stack development sprint
