About TechFusion 2025

TechFusion 2025 is a dynamic two-day extravaganza dedicated to fueling the innovative spirit of B.Tech Computer Science students and technology enthusiasts. It is designed to be the ultimate hub for creativity and collaboration, bringing together coding aficionados, AI/ML pioneers, cybersecurity wizards, blockchain enthusiasts, and budding tech entrepreneurs under one roof.

Whether you are passionate about crafting mobile applications, developing immersive games, delving into blockchain technology, mastering the art of data science, or strengthening your expertise in cybersecurity, this event has something tailored just for you.

Key Highlights

  • Hackathons and Coding Challenges: Test your programming skills and solve real-world problems in competitive hackathons.
  • Workshops and Training Sessions: Hands-on workshops on AI, ML, game development, blockchain, AR, and VR led by industry experts.
  • Tech Talks and Keynotes: Learn from thought leaders about the latest trends in technology, including AI ethics, cybersecurity, and quantum computing.
  • Panel Discussions and Networking: Connect with like-minded individuals and share perspectives in engaging panel discussions.
  • Startup Showcase and Ideathon: Present your innovative ideas to investors and mentors, with opportunities for funding and guidance.
  • Gaming and Robotics Competitions: Showcase your skills in game design and robotics challenges.
  • Exhibition of Projects and Innovations: Explore a variety of inspiring projects and innovations displayed by students and professionals.
  • Recruitment and Internship Drives: Interact with leading tech companies and explore career opportunities.
